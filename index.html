Our Story

Pencil, paper, patience

Thulasi Verse is an independent portrait studio devoted to the slow craft of pencil and graphite. Every commission begins with a conversation — your story, the moment you want to keep, the people you love — and ends with an heirloom you can hold in your hands.

With over 8 years of practice and 400+ commissions delivered to homes across India, Singapore, the UK and Australia, our work has been chosen for weddings, memorials, milestone birthdays and corporate gifts.
